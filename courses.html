Empirical Analysis for Policymakers

Sciences Po Paris · Fall 2024

Introduces inferential statistical methods and econometric/regression analysis tools essential for understanding empirical public policy and planning research. Uses R extensively, with readings from Angrist & Pischke's Mastering 'Metrics.
